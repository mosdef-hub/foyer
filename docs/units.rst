=====
Units
=====

Foyer uses the following units:

+----------+---------+
| Quantity |  Units  |
+==========+=========+
| distance |    nm   |
+----------+---------+
|   angle  | radians |
+----------+---------+
|   mass   |   amu   |
+----------+---------+
|  energy  |  kJ/mol |
+----------+---------+

See also `mBuild unit documentation <https://mbuild.mosdef.org/en/stable/units.html>`_ and `OpenMM unit documentation <http://docs.openmm.org/latest/userguide/theory.html#units>`_.
